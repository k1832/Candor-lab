# Adversarial review #1 of design 0012 (structured concurrency) — REJECTED FOR REWORK

**Date:** 2026-07-09. **Verdict: (c)** — the first design-tier rejection in the project's
history, at design twelve. The central claim ("races unrepresentable in safe code") was
falsified by construction: a copy-with-rawptr type (the Alloc handle, the design's own flagship)
crossed via read-borrow then copied out gives two tasks one mutable free list with zero unsafe.
The flagship split_mut parallel-fill does not type-check under the stated rules (three-rule
collision); no result-return mechanism exists though the design relies on one; the §12 proof's
stated reasoning ("join is the sole sw edge") is false (spawn edges and nested scopes are
interior sync ops) though the conclusion is salvageable with per-edge truncation pinning.

Dispositions (deciding authority's session) — all ten accepted; rework directions:
1. **The spawn-crossing rule unified:** every spawn argument is gated — take requires the type
portable; read/write borrows cross as scope-length loans (resolving the finding-2 collision
explicitly as the stated exemption) but their REFERENT type must be portable (no rawptr
reachable, transitively, including through copy fields). This kills the Alloc laundering and
legalizes slice_mut-of-portable-elements crossing.
2. split_mut re-characterized honestly: a compiler-blessed primitive with a stipulated
disjointness postcondition over an unsafe body — a new blessed exception, priced, not "the loan
scan falling out" (the scan is index-insensitive by 0001's own rule).
3. **Results return via write-borrowed slots, stated as THE mechanism**, with §3.1's containment
rewritten: pre-fault in-place mutations ARE visible post-join and window-interior
(build-mode-dependent) — named loudly per finding 7.
4. §12: the per-edge pin — truncation before EVERY spawn/join sync op, the proof via Thm 1
applied per edge; O2 provable only after fix 1 closes the laundering channel.
5. Scope-body faults specified (cancel-then-join); unbounded-but-legal fault delivery at the
join named loudly. 6. Stage-2 honesty: the gate presupposes DRF, never establishes it; the
checker alone carries race-freedom. 7. portable exported in interface signatures (0007/0008
hook); alloc crosses spawn. 8. stdout multi-writer recorded as an explicit P5
declared-nondeterminism extension. 9-10. As proposed.

**What held:** the keyword-over-library argument, cooperative cancellation, atomics deferral,
spawn-order-first fault identity, the per-task-arena idiom, O4, and the NN#13 walk.
Rework returns to review (the failed-retest discipline applies at design tier too).
